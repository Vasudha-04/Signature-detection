About SignatureGuard

Revolutionizing document security with cutting-edge AI technology

Our Mission

We're dedicated to providing the most reliable and accurate signature verification technology to protect businesses and individuals from fraud.

SignatureGuard leverages state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to analyze and verify handwritten signatures with unprecedented accuracy. Our system is designed to meet the needs of financial institutions, legal firms, healthcare providers, and any organization that requires secure document authentication.

Model Architecture

  • VGG16 Base: Pre-trained on ImageNet
  • Transfer Learning: 14.7M frozen parameters
  • Custom Layers: Dense(256) + Softmax output
  • Input Size: 224×224×3 RGB images

Technical Specifications

VGG16 Architecture

Based on the renowned VGG16 convolutional neural network with 16 layers, pre-trained on ImageNet dataset for robust feature extraction.

Training Dataset

Trained on Kaggle's Signature Verification Dataset with 1,649 samples, including genuine and forged signatures across multiple subjects.

Performance Metrics

Achieved 98% test accuracy with 95.6% validation accuracy after 20 epochs using Adam optimizer and categorical cross-entropy loss.
